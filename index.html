About — The Person Behind the Work

Curious, hands-on, and obsessed with building things that actually make sense.

The Short Version

I design digital products, brand experiences, and interactive systems that bridge the gap between ideas and execution. From core app interfaces to growth surfaces like social, PR, and experiments, I focus on clarity, usability, and impact over decoration.

This portfolio also reflects my work in computer science — projects where design meets logic, performance, and real-world constraints. Whether it's a visual system, a web experience, or a coded prototype, I enjoy taking ideas all the way from concept to implementation.

“Good design isn't loud — it's intentional, effective, and quietly powerful.”

When I'm not designing or building, I'm usually exploring visual inspiration, refining concepts, or thinking about how products and systems fit into real lives.

02 — Philosophy

What I believe

01

Craft Over Trend

Trends expire. Craft compounds. I build systems and experiences that age like architecture, not fashion.

02

Motion Is Meaning

Animation isn't decoration — it's communication. Every transition, every reveal carries emotional weight and narrative purpose.

03

Details Are Decisions

The space between elements, the curve of a corner, the weight of a font — these aren't afterthoughts. They're the work.

04 — Systems

I think in components

Reusable parts. Predictable states. Intentional constraints. The system is the design.
